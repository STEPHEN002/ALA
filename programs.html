Our Core Programs

Four integrated program areas targeting the root causes of poverty and vulnerability across Uganda.

Systematic Focus

Integrated Community Development

Our programs are designed to strengthen community resilience, improve access to opportunities, and empower local people to take ownership of their development processes. Each area is interconnected — education enables livelihoods, health enables education, and the environment sustains them all.

📚
Program 1

Child Development & Education

Focus: Ending child poverty through access to quality education and holistic support.

  • Child sponsorship programs covering education, nutrition, and protection
  • Support to schools — learning materials, infrastructure, teacher support
  • AgriSTEM initiatives: school gardens and agricultural learning for sustainability
  • Early childhood development (ECD) programs
  • Scholarships and retention programs for vulnerable children
  • Child protection and rights awareness campaigns
💼
Program 2

Youth Empowerment, Vocational Skills & Entrepreneurship

Focus: Equipping young people and women with practical skills and leadership capacity.

  • Vocational training in tailoring, carpentry, ICT, mechanics, and more
  • Entrepreneurship development and small business support
  • Financial literacy and savings groups (VSLA models)
  • Leadership and civic engagement training
  • Innovation hubs and mentorship programs
  • Job readiness and employability skills
🏥
Program 3

Health, HIV/AIDS Awareness & Community Wellbeing

Focus: Improving health outcomes through prevention, education, and access.

  • HIV/AIDS awareness, prevention, and stigma reduction
  • Sexual and reproductive health education (SRHR)
  • Maternal and child health support
  • Community health outreach and screenings
  • Nutrition programs for children and mothers
  • Water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) initiatives
  • Mental health and psychosocial support
🌱
Program 4

Environment, Agriculture & Climate Resilience

Focus: Promoting sustainable livelihoods and environmental protection.

  • Climate-smart agriculture and farmer training
  • Tree planting and reforestation programs
  • Sustainable land use and soil conservation
  • Food security and household resilience programs
  • Renewable energy promotion — clean cooking and solar solutions
  • Community awareness on climate change adaptation
Additional Focus Areas

Disability Inclusion & Research

Two cross-cutting priorities embedded across all our programs.

♿

Disability Inclusion

Promoting inclusion and equal opportunities for persons with disabilities through accessible services, inclusive education, livelihood opportunities, and strengthened community-based protection and advocacy systems.

🔬

Research, Innovation & Learning

Generating evidence-based solutions and strengthening program effectiveness through research, data collection, innovation, and knowledge sharing that informs policy and improves interventions.
